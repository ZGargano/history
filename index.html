1894 Class Stone

As one of the oldest recorded class stones in this schools history, it's not surprising that it would have gone missing. We don't have many clue as to its location, but here is what we do have:

  • The ceremony was held under "rustling trees"
  • The other class stones around this year are located in the middle, grassy area of campus
  • Stones around this time period were also basic and square shaped, so this one may be the same.

1895 Class Stone

Just like 1894's stone, there aren't many clue towards its location, but here is what we do know:

  • The ceremony was held next to "tall trees"
  • Just like before, the other class stones around this time period are located in the middle of campus
  • Also like before, this stone may be square shaped as well.
