1894 Class Stone

As one of the oldest recorded class stones in this schools history, it's not surprising that it would have gone missing. We don't have many clue as to its location, but here is what we do have:

  • The ceremony was held under "rustling trees"
  • The other class stones around this year are located in the middle, grassy area of campus

1895 Class Stone
